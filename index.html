North Star Lobster Company

At North Star Lobster Co. seek to make the valuable, yet never ending, process of buying and transporting bait and lobster a much faster and easier process. We strive to maintain competitive bait prices, and offer delivery of bait as well as transportation lobster to the distributor. This allows you as the fishermen to focus more on fishing, while we focus on keeping you stocked with bait and pick up and transport your lobster.

Some of the services that we provide are:
- Fishing bait
- Bait Delivery
- Lobster Pick-up & Delivery
